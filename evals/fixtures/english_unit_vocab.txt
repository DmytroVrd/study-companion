Topic: English vocabulary and exercises

This unit contains English language exercises for vocabulary and grammar practice. Students
complete sentences, choose correct words, match sentence parts, and correct mistakes.

Vocabulary to learn:
- election: a public vote to choose a leader or government
- protest: a public action showing disagreement
- vote: to choose in an election
- application: a formal request
- profession: a job that needs special training
- ambition: a strong wish to achieve something
- contract: a legal agreement
- benefit: an advantage or useful result
- affect: to influence something
- tight: fitting closely
- loose: not tight
- rough: not smooth
- smooth: even and pleasant to touch
- climate: typical weather conditions
- environment: the natural world around us
- heatwave: a period of very hot weather
