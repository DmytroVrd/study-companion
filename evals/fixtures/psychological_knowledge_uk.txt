Topic: Relevance of psychological knowledge in the modern world

Psychological knowledge is an understanding of how people think, feel, and interact with
each other. It helps explain behavior, emotional reactions, communication, motivation, and
decision-making.

Psychology helps people know themselves better, understand others, build healthier
relationships, cope with stress, and make more balanced decisions. During war, crisis, and
rapid social change, psychological knowledge becomes especially important because it helps
people recognize anxiety, support others, and maintain emotional stability.
